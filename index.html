Biography

Mingfei is a Senior Research Scientist at the Salesforce Research, Palo Alto, USA. She received her PhD degree from the Computer Science Department at the University of Maryland College Park, advised by Prof. Larry S. Davis. Before that, she obtained her bachelor degree and master degree from the Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications. Her research interests include action recognition, object detection, weakly supervised learning and documentAI.
